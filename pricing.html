Catering Pricing in Nagpur

Formula: (Base Price + Menu Add-ons) × Headcount − Discounts + Surcharges + Distance Charges

Base price

₹280 per plate standard service inclusion.

Discounts & surcharges

  • 5% discount for 100+ guests
  • 10% discount for 200+ guests
  • 8% surcharge for weddings

Distance charges

First 10 km included, then ₹18/km.

Example calculation

120 guests, menu add-on ₹200/plate, wedding, 18 km away:
(₹280 + ₹200) × 120 = ₹57,600
Discount 5% = ₹2,880
Surcharge 8% = ₹4,608
Distance = ₹144
Total = ₹59,472
